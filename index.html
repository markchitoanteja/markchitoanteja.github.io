Installation & Billing Information

Installation Fee

A one-time installation fee of ₱1,500 is required for every new internet connection.

1 Month Free Internet

The installation fee is separate from your one (1) month free internet service. The free month remains a promotional benefit and is not applied toward the installation cost.

Billing Schedule

Your regular monthly billing will begin one (1) month after the official installation date. This allows you to enjoy your complimentary first month before your first payment becomes due.
